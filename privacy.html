Privacy Policy for Level Up – College Prep

Effective Date: September 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Level Up (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a personalized college preparation experience. This includes:

  • Account Information: Email address (used for authentication and account confirmation) and password (created by the user).
  • Profile Information: Name, high school, grade, areas of interest, awards, extracurricular activities, GPA, SAT/ACT scores, and courses taken.
  • App Activity: Assignments and calendar events you create in the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Authenticate and manage your account.
  • Store and display your assignments, events, and academic information.
  • Provide personalized recommendations for extracurricular activities and programs.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.

4. Data Storage

All personal and app-related data is stored securely in Firebase. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except to comply with legal requirements or work with trusted service providers under confidentiality agreements.

6. User Choices

You can:

  • Delete your account and personal information at any time by contacting us.
  • Update or correct the information in your profile through the app.

7. Children’s Privacy

Level Up is intended for middle and high school students. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
